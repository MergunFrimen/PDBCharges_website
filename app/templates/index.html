PDBCharges

PDBCharges is a web application providing partial atomic charges of protein structures from the Protein Data Bank. The charges are computed by the the semiempirical quantum mechanical methods GFN1-xTB and reproduce the PBE0/TZVP/CM5 charges. Before computation of the charges, hydrogens are added to the structure by MoleculeKit and TODO at pH 7.2. The details about the methodology and usage are described in the TODO. This website is free and open to all users and there is no login requirement. Source codes are freely available at GitHub TODO (jaké dát odkazy když jsou dva?).
